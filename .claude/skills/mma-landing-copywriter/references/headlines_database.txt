## Headline Testing Priority

### High-Converting Patterns:
1. **Number + Timeframe + Result**: "Get Fight-Ready in 12 Weeks"
2. **Identity Transformation**: "From [Current State] to [Desired State]"
3. **Specific Benefit + Urgency**: "Learn Self-Defense Before You Need It"
4. **Social Proof + Benefit**: "Join 500+ Members Who've Already Transformed"
5. **Problem + Solution**: "Tired of Boring Workouts? Learn to Fight"

### A/B Testing Matrix:
- **Emotional vs. Rational**: Appeal to feelings vs. facts
- **Specific vs. General**: Concrete numbers vs. broad promises
- **You-Focused vs. We-Focused**: Customer benefits vs. gym features
- **Question vs. Statement**: Engage curiosity vs. make claims

### Psychology Integration:
Each headline should trigger at least one psychological driver:
- **Identity**: Who the customer wants to become
- **Fear**: Pain they want to avoid
- **Social Proof**: What others are doing
- **Scarcity**: Limited opportunity
- **Authority**: Credibility and expertise
- **Reciprocity**: Value-first approach
- **Commitment**: Small steps toward bigger goals
